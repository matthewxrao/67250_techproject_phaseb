MonoMuse is dedicated to bridging the worlds of art, technology, and human creativity. Our mission is to inspire curiosity, spark imagination, and make contemporary digital art accessible to everyone.

We serve artists, students, families, educators, and anyone curious about how technology is reshaping creative expression. From interactive installations to community workshops, MonoMuse is a space for all.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, MonoMuse has grown from a small pop-up gallery into a landmark cultural institution. Here are some of our key milestones:

  • 2018 - MonoMuse founded as a pop-up digital art gallery in Pittsburgh
  • 2019 - Opened our permanent location with three exhibition halls
  • 2020 - Launched virtual tours, reaching over 50,000 online visitors
  • 2022 - Welcomed our 500,000th in-person visitor
  • 2024 - Expanded with a new interactive technology wing
  • 2025 - Partnered with Carnegie Mellon University for educational programming
