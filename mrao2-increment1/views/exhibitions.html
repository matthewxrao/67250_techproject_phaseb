Exhibitions

Digital Horizons: Art Beyond the Screen

Step into a world where pixels meet paint. Digital Horizons showcases works by emerging artists who blend traditional techniques with cutting-edge digital tools, creating immersive environments that challenge our perception of reality.

The Machine Garden

An interactive installation where visitors walk through a living landscape of kinetic sculptures and AI-generated soundscapes. The Machine Garden explores the relationship between nature and technology, inviting you to plant digital seeds and watch them grow in real time.

Echoes of Light

A mesmerizing exhibition of projection-mapped sculptures that respond to sound and movement. Created by artist collective LumenLab, Echoes of Light transforms the gallery into a dynamic canvas of color, shadow, and rhythm.
